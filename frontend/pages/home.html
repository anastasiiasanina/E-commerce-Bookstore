Delivery And Shipping

We offer fast and reliable delivery services for all orders placed on our website.

Shipping Options

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery within 5-7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Delivery within 2-3 business days. Additional charges apply.
  • International Shipping: Delivery time varies based on the destination.

Shipping Rates

Our shipping rates are based on the total weight of the books in your order and the selected shipping method. The exact shipping cost will be calculated during the checkout process.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order is shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to track the status of your delivery on our website.

Returns and Refunds

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the books within 30 days of delivery. Please refer to our Returns Policy for more information on how to initiate a return and request a refund.
